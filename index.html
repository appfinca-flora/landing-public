Stay Focused, Together

     Get on App Store

Flora helps you and friends put down the phones and be present.

1.

Plant a seed with friends whenever you want to put down the phones.

2.

As you focus on what‘s more important, the seed will gradually grow into a healthy tree.

3.

But if anyone can‘t resist using the phone, the tree will be killed.

Use Flora when...

studying together

or in a class or meeting room

or at anytime you wish to enjoy the real life.
