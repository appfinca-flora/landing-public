Garden Ideas

Each tree in your garden lasts for 7 days. So, by strategically selecting plants to grow, you can have a completely new look in your garden at the end of every week. Trust us, it’s very satisfying to see your hard work visualized in a way you want. Get started by following these 3 steps:

  1. At the beginning of a week, come out with a new landscaping idea based on anything such as plant colors, locations, types, or your moods, etc.

  2. In the next 7 days, press the Seed icon on the main screen to select and grow trees following your idea.

  3. Stick with your plan and be surprised by your lovely garden at the end of the week!

Flora will take a Snapshot of your amazing work at the end of every week. You can share it with your friends and family to challenge each to other .

Be Reminded and Motivated

As in the main app, each tree in the Flora widget lasts for 7 days. This means that you have to plant trees regularly to keep the widget full of life. When seeing fewer and fewer trees on the Home screen, you know it’s time to grow a new one and do your work.

If you chose a large Flora widget, it also shows your to-dos, with the upcoming one highlighted on top. You will never forget important things again because they are sitting right on your Home screen. Besides, each to-do shows the progress you’ve made and reminds you of how far you’re away from your goal.

With a Flora widget, you can boost your productivity to a whole new level. Please give it a try and let us know what you think via the in-app feedback system.

Q: 我的小工具裡什麼也沒顯示。該怎麼辦？

小工具大約每 30 分鐘會與主 app 同步一次。所以，請給它一些時間。如果你等了很長的時間後卻仍然沒有看見任何內容，請

  1. 將 Flora app 更新到最新版本

  2. 打開 app 並前往你的 花園 (必要步驟)

  3. 回到 桌面 將小工具移除，並重新加入

小工具的內容應在接下來的 30 分鐘內顯示。如果問題仍然持續，請通過 app 內的反饋系統與我們聯繫。我們將協助您解決問題。
