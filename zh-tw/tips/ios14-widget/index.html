Flora Widgets for iOS 14

Productivity right on your Home screen.

Always forget to plant trees or your to-dos? No worry. Flora widgets get you covered. By adding a Flora widget on the Home screen, you will be reminded of your trees and to-dos whenever you go.

Adding a Widget

You can add a Flora widget by following 3 simple steps:

  1. Long press the Home screen and then tap on the Add (+) button at the upper-left corner.

  2. Scroll down in the widget list and choose Flora.

  3. Among different widget sizes, select the one that fits your needs and then press Add Widget.

Voilà! You have just turned your Home screen into a lovely garden. From now on, the Flora widget will periodically sync with the main app to display the trees that you have grown right on your Home screen. The syncing takes place about every 30 minutes. So, if you do not see your trees immediately, please wait for a while.

Be Reminded and Motivated

As in the main app, each tree in the Flora widget lasts for 7 days. This means that you have to plant trees regularly to maintain a beautiful garden in the widget. When you see fewer and fewer trees, it’s time to grow a new one by putting down your phone and working on the tasks at hand.

If choosing a Flora widget of the largest size, you can also see your to-dos, with the upcoming one highlighted on top. Never forget a thing anymore! Besides, the widget shows the progress you’ve made so you always have a clear picture of how far you’re away from your goals.
