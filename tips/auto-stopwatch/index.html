Make Every Minute Count with Auto-stopwatch

More precise time tracking for more work.

It can be frustrating when you set a Focus timer for a specific task, work on it for longer than expected, and end up finding that only the time set in the timer was counted and logged in the stats and a story. With Flora’s new Auto-stopwatch feature, you can now record the extra time you spend on a task, giving you a more accurate reflection of your productivity.

How It Works

First, please update your Flora app to version 3.5 or above. Then you can enjoy the new feature when growing a tree.

After starting a focus timer, you’ll see a “Focus Duration Indicator” next to it. This indicator starts counting up when the Focus timer runs out (see ), and Flora won’t stop the session immediately. Instead, it will let the indicator accumulate the extra time you spend on the task, even after the timer has expired.

The session only ends when you press the “See Tree” button, and Flora will record the total focus duration, including the time you spent beyond the original timer. This way, you won't miss any extra time you spend on your task, and you can accurately track your productivity.

We believe this new feature will help you stay focused and motivated, knowing that all the time you spend on your task is accurately recorded. Plus, it's a great way to challenge yourself to get work done before the break.

Q: What if I finish my work earlier than expected?

If you finish a task before the end of a Focus timer, simply hit the “Pause” button to take a break and switch to other apps as needed. Once the timer is up, you’ll receive a notification prompting you to return to Flora and see your tree. The time you spent on pause will be deducted from the Focus Duration Indicator before it starts accumulating the extra time. By working in tandem, the Auto-stopwatch and Pause features ensure that your daily productivity is accurately measured no matter what.
