FAQs

What should I write or say?

Anything you like (or dislike) about Flora and why people should download it. Usually, first-hand experience with personal touches will make a compelling story.

What's the reward?

We are not a rich company, but we are happy to help you get motivated all the time by offering a free LIFETIME subscription to our Flora Care service. Depending on the quality of your work, we will activate your Flora Care Standard, Accelerated, or Express lifetime plan, which is worth $120, $240, or $600, respectively. If you have already activated Flora Care, we will upgrade your plan.

Flora Care is a service that lets you plan a real tree every time when your total focus time reaches your goal. For more details, please swipe your Garden view to the right to go to the Flora Care view. Then press the Learn More button.

What are the requirements?

To be eligible for the reward, your article or video should

  • mention Flora and link to our website at https://flora.appfinca.com.

  • be original, non-spammy and do not violate our Terms of Service.

How to get my reward?

Please send us the URL of your work via the in-app feedback system or email at support@flora.appfinca.com. We will review your work in 3 business days and activate your lifetime Flora Care subscription if everything looks fine.
