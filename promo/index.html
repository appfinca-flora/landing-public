FAQs

What should I write or say?

Anything you like (or dislike) about Flora and why people should download it. Usually, first-hand experience with personal touches will make a compelling story.

What's the reward?

We are not a rich company, but we are happy to strengthen your commitment to the Earth and our rural communities by activating your Flora Care EXPRESS plan for free for 1 to 3 years, depending on the quality of your work. If you have already activated Flora Care, we will upgrade your plan.

Flora Care EXPRESS is a service that lets you plan a real tree every time when your total focus time reaches 24 hours. For more details, please swipe your Garden view to the right to go to the Flora Care view. Then press the Learn More button.

How to make sure if I will get the reward?

To be eligible for the reward, your article or video must

  • have its own URL; posts on social media sites such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter are not eligible for the reward.

  • mention Flora and link to our website at https://flora.appfinca.com.

  • be original, non-spammy and do not violate our Terms of Service.

How to get my reward?

Please send us the URL of your work via the in-app feedback system or email at support@flora.appfinca.com. We will review it and activate your Flora Care EXPRESS in 3 business days if everything looks fine.
