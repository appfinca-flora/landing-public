Flora Impact Report, 2021

A wonderful journey with you.

Happy New Year! Every year, we are blown away by our passionate users. Last year was no different, thanks to you.

You worked hard in 2021, and you proved that the little things – one minute of focus, one little tree in your Garden, one to-do completion – really add up. Through the Flora “Price” and “Care” services, your effort was converted into strong support for the farmers in our partnering rural communities to plant real trees, thereby creating jobs, protecting ecosystems, and helping mitigate climate change.

Together, Flora users have planted 76,391 real trees on the planet. The planting tasks were handled by more than 3,808 of our partnering farmers in Mali, Chad, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania. About 250 acres of farmland were restored.

Let’s dig into some details behind these amazing numbers.

New partner

Today, we are thrilled to announce a new partnership with Eden Reforestation Projects, a non-profit organization based in California, USA. This enriches our partnering rural communities and strengthens our commitment to reducing extreme poverty and restoring healthy forests.

Among the 76,391 real trees planted, more than 31,000 were fulfilled by Eden Reforestation Projects. Eden employs tens of thousands of people worldwide to plant real trees. Through the partnership, Flora users have provided necessary jobs, tools, and education for the families in the following new countries:

  • “Madagascar”
  • “Indonesia”
  • “Nepal”
  • “Mozambique”
  • “Haiti”
  • “Honduras”
  • “Nicaragua”
  • “Brazil”

People we helped

We supported the families in our partnering rural communities by financing the perma-gardening projects. These projects train farmers living in poverty over multiple years, during which the farmers learn how to protect and diversify their land without harmful and expensive chemical inputs. By the end of the program, the farming family is making a reliable income and eating regular nutritional meals.

Your continuous use of Flora has provided lasting aid for our planet and vulnerable communities in need. Many people’s lives have been changed, forever.

I am grateful for the knowledge we are getting to make our farming more profitable... to provide for my five children and nine-person household.

— Waneloba Loy, a teacher from Uganda

Now I have something to eat and sell to support my family during the dry season. I consider it as the only chance to maintain my family’s happiness.

— Hamis Ramadhani from Tanzania

The real trees we helped plant will continue providing food or resources to these farmers for years to come and, at the same time, they’re restoring our environment by bringing back biodiversity, channeling water into the ground, improving soil fertility, and storing carbon.

All the above could not have been done without the hard work and determination of Flora users. We are so proud of you.

Outlook for 2022

As Flora developers, we will continue our mission to create a better place to live while improving the Flora app. And thanks to our incredible supporters like you, we have been growing at an average annual rate of 50% over the past two years. This means we can grow more real trees and reach more families, faster!

However, due to a changing climate and the impacts of COVID-19, our mission is getting more and more challenging. You can continue to make a difference and keep us on the pathway to a thriving planet and humanity by simply enabling Flora “Price” during a focus session or subscribing to the “Care” service.

Thank you again. Let’s keep up the good work and make a productive 2022!
