Flora Impact Report, 2020

A wonderful journey with you.

To all our beloved Flora users: happy New Year! We can’t thank you enough for supporting the Flora app and helping our partnering rural communities remain resilient in the face of adversity. Last year has been incredibly challenging for the communities. But, thanks to you, farmers in our program are able to plant real trees and produce what they need to improve their circumstances while making the earth greener.

Today, we are happy to share some statistics of what we have changed to the environment in the past years. It’s a memorable step in our mission to create a better place to live while developing the Flora app.

50,927

real trees planted.

169

acres farmland restored.

1,523

people helped.

Through the Flora Price and Care services, Flora users have planted 50,927 real trees on the planet. The planting tasks were handled by more than 1,523 of our partnering rural farmers in Kenya, Senegal, Tanzania, and Uganda. About 170 acres of farmland were restored.

We supported the farmers by financing the perma-gardening projects run by some international NPOs, such as the Trees for the Future. These organizations train farmers living in poverty over multiple years, during which the farmers learn how to protect and diversify their land without harmful and expensive chemical inputs. By the end of the program, the farming family is making a reliable income and eating regular nutritional meals.

Your continuous use of Flora has provided lasting aid for our planet and vulnerable communities in need. Many people’s lives have been changed, forever.

I’ve learned the importance of protecting my land, growing agroforestry seedlings, intercropping, and land revitalization techniques like alley cropping and contour farming.

— Salma Mussa, a Tanzanian farmer

The vegetables in my garden have really helped my family, they are feeding us and I am selling some of them at the market. The perma-garden project has really helped us … as a community, it is our project.

— Francis Adupa from Kole, Uganda

The real trees we helped plant will continue providing food or resources to these farmers for years to come and, at the same time, they’re restoring our environment by bringing back biodiversity, channeling water into the ground, improving soil fertility, and storing carbon.

All the above could not have been done without the hard work and determination of Flora users. We are so proud of you.

Outlook for 2021

Thanks to our incredible community of supporters, we have been growing at an average annual rate of 50% over the past two years. This means we can grow more real trees and reach more families, faster!

However, due to a changing climate and the impacts of COVID-19, our mission is getting more and more challenging. If you want to continue to make a difference and keep us on the pathway to a thriving planet and humanity, please enable Flora Price or subscribe to the Care services.

Thank you again. Let’s keep up the good work and make a productive 2021!
