Unlocking Your Potential with App Whitelist

The power to focus on your terms.

As you know, Flora helps you stay on task and avoid distractions by blocking other apps while you are growing trees. However, we recognize that some of you need to use certain apps, such as a calculator or dictionary app, to complete your tasks effectively. That’s why we’ve developed a whitelist feature that allows you to use some apps during a Focus session without killing your tree.

How Does It Work

To use this new feature, please upgrade your iOS to version 16.1+ and Flora app to version 3.5+. Then, open the app and follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the “More” tab and turn on the “Allow apps during Focus” option.

  2. Grant Flora access to Screen Time on your device.

  3. Tap on the “Allow all apps” option below. From there, you can select the apps you want to use during your Focus sessions.

Now, when growing a tree, if you try to open an app that’s not in your whitelist, you'll see a shield screen telling you to go back to the iOS Home screen. This is a reminder that we’re here to help you stay focused on your tasks, and we want to make sure you’re aware of any distractions that may be tempting you away from your goals.

Q: When should I enable app whitelist?

For many users, Flora’s default app-blocking behavior works just right to help them stay focused in today’s digital age, where phones are the major source of distractions. However, if you work with your phone and need certain apps to complete your task, consider enabling the whitelist feature.

Q: Can the whitelist feature guarantee that my tree will never die during a Focus session?

No. When the whitelist feature is enabled, you will kill a tree if you try to cancel an ongoing Focus session by, for example, killing the Flora app. Therefore, it’s still important to be mindful and committed to your Focus session in order to successfully grow your tree.
